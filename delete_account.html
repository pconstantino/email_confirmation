Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from within the Our Nest app.

How to delete your account

1
Open the Our Nest app and sign in
2
Tap the Profile tab (bottom right)
3
Scroll down to the settings section
4
Tap Delete Account and confirm

Data that will be deleted

Your account and login credentials
Your couple profile and partner names
All question sessions and answers
Mood entries and history
Badges, streaks, and challenge completions

Can't access the app? Contact us at
support@ournest.app and we'll delete your account manually.
